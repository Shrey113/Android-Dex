Getting Started

Quick Setup

Up and running in under 5 minutes

01

Enable Wireless Debugging

Go to Developer Options on your Android device and enable "Wireless debugging". Note down the IP address and port shown.

02

Launch the Desktop App

Download and run Android DEX on your Windows, macOS, or Linux computer. The app will start the local server automatically.

03

Connect Your Device

Enter your phone's IP address in the app or select it from the discovered devices list. Click "Start DEX" to begin.

04

Enjoy Desktop Mode

A new window will open with your extended display. Use your mouse and keyboard to control the desktop environment.
